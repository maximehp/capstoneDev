Forgejo & Runners

  • Forgejo acts as the self-hosted Git source of truth.
  • Act Runners execute workflows triggered by Forgejo events.
  • Secrets and variables are injected at runtime through Forgejo secret management.
  • ChirpLabs was designed as a shared environment with team isolation in repos, NAS datasets, secrets, and variables.
