Networking / Overview

Expanded Segmentation, Stable Connectivity, VM-ready VLANs

The networking work moved the lab from a smaller addressing plan into a larger, segmented design that Proxmox VMs can use directly.

Addressing Expanded the overall subnet from /24 to /21 for a larger usable IP range.
Segmentation Moved from six original VLAN groups to seven working VLAN groups.
Proxmox SDN Used OVS bonds, bridges, static VM IPs, and VLAN tags for proper placement.
Operations Resolved connectivity issues and identified firewall, policy, and alerting improvements.
