Infrastructure - IaC / CI-CD

Forgejo & Runners

  • Forgejo acts as the self-hosted Git source of truth.
  • Act Runners execute workflows triggered by Forgejo events.
  • Secrets and variables are injected at runtime through Forgejo secret management.
  • ChirpLabs was designed as a shared environment with team isolation in repos, NAS datasets, secrets, and variables.

Forgejo

Self-hosted Git platform serving as the source of truth for all repositories and CI/CD triggers.

Act Runners

Container-based workflow executors that run CI/CD jobs triggered by Forgejo events.

Secrets & Variables

Secure runtime injection of encrypted secrets and environment variables for workflows.
